Module 14 - Sensors

Overview

Mobile devices often contain sensors to report linear and angular acceleration, compass orientation, pressure, temperature, light and other environmental conditions.

In this module, we'll explore some of the common sensors that exist on most devices and use these sensors to drive a simple Augmented Reality (AR) Heads-Up Display (HUD).

Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Determine which sensors are available on a device.
  • Read the values of sensors in an Activity.
  • Determine the device orientation using sensor fusion.
  • Use a Camera Preview to display what the camera is capturing.
  • Draw graphics on top of the Camera Preview.
  • Modify the graphics based on device orientation.
