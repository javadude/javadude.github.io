Module 0 - Course Logistics

Overview

Welcome to 605.486!

In this module I'll attempt to scare you off so I have fewer assignments to grade by discussing the course schedule, office hours, grading policy and the ways you can use the course site to communicate with each other and me.

Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Determine the schedule by which you should complete modules in this course.
  • Attend office hours (optional but encouraged) to directly communicate with the instructor.
  • Submit (future module) assignments that adhere to the course grading policies.
  • Locate the proper communication mechanisms on the course site for discussion and instructor contact.

Learning Guide

Begin this module by watching all of the recorded lectures below. I strongly recommend that you watch them in the order listed, as some lectures assume knowledge gained from earlier lectures.

Refer to the Course Outline for any assignment start or due dates!

Please post any questions to the Q & A section of the Discussion Board.

Notes

  • Most weeks require completion of more than one course module. It's important that you familiarize yourself with the course schedule by reviewing the Course Outline so you know which modules are expected to be completed each week.

  • Explore the course web site. Pay particular attention to the Course Schedule and Course Grading Policy pages! The grading policy contains details on what I expect for all assignment submissions, and I talk about the major points in the video.
