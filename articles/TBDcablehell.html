My Cable Hell

Thursday 8/19/99

Cable installed on time

Picture is snowy on half the channels
The lawn was marked by MISSDIG/MISSUTILITY crews for digging before we moved in, but noone ever came to do the digging.
We called several times to complain about the picture and they finally said they'd send someone 9/15. 

Wednesday 9/15/99

NO SHOW - we blew this off at first as I planned to call about a cable modem the next week when I got back from Omaha.

Thursday 9/23/99

Picked up cable modem from office. It had a $10 "self-install kit" which consisted of a few pieces of paper stapled together listing the servers, IP, etc, the modem, a splitter and two segments of cable. They call that an "install kit" and have the nerve to charge me $10???

Thursday 9/23/99

Signal's too weak to use the modem. So I called and they said they'd send someone Tuesday (9/28) to check it out and possibly add an amplifier.

Tuesday 9/28/99 2:15p

I call to ask where the Hell the tech is. They say he's running late, probably be another hour. They confirmed that I definitely was scheduled.

Tuesday 9/28/99 4:00p

I call to ask again, they have no clue where the tech is. Say they can reschedule for tomorrow (Wed 9/29 9-12). She explicitly said "tomorrow".

Wednesday 9/29/99 9:15a

I call to confirm the appt. Turns out I'm not on the schedule until THURSDAY 9/30. I ask to speak to supervisors and complain, but they say "Sorry there's no way we can get a tech there today"

Thursday 9/30/99 9:45a

Tech shows up. Turns out MAIN line for the neighborhood is too weak. (If this is the case they HAD to be getting LOTS of calls from people in the neighborhood RE snowy picture...)
They need to send another tech, but he won't need to come inside. I sign the work order, and notice the "on time guarantee" with a $20 credit if they're late"

Thursday 9/30/99 10:45a

I call to get a $60 credit for 3 late appts. The idiot on the phone doesn't have any record that I ever had an appt on Tuesday. She did everything except explicitly call me a liar about having the Tuesday appt!

Thursday 9/30/99 11:30a

Talked with Gabrielle (x283) who was actually helpful -- she FOUND the 9/28 appt and was the first person who actually seemed helpful/empathetic

Thursday 9/30/99 11:45a

called to talk w/ a supervisor (Sharon?)
RE credits. She gave me credit for 9/28 after fighting for a while but basically called me a liar for 9/15 and 9/29. She said she would research and call me back. (She said she'll talk with the person I talked with yesterday and would call back -- no returned call. Turns out I found on on Sunday 10/3/99 that she had given me the credit but not bothered to call me back so I could say "told you so")
I stated there are two possibilities:

  1. record for appt was never entered
  2. record was lost or deleted

She stated there was a third possibility (implying that I am lying). Status was a "line referral" was put in, and the problem is supposed to be resolved in 48 hours.

Saturday 10/2/99 11:30a

I call to check the status as it's been over 48 hours. Person on the phone says it's scheduled for today and should be complete by 8pm.

Saturday 10/2/99 3pm

I talk with my next-door neighbor. He had cable two years ago and the signal was very poor. When he called to complain they said "at least three people must call or it's not a problem". He put up with it for a year (as well as many frustrating calls with their "customer service") then dropped it. Picture has been bad at least two years in this neighborhood.

Saturday 10/2/99 8pm

still not enough signal

Sun 10/3/99 9:30a

Call to check status. She says scheduled for today and should be completed by 8pm. Further, she says it was originally scheduled for 10/5, but the techs were able to move it to TODAY and there was NEVER any schedule for it yesterday.

Sun 10/3/99 9:40a (15min) RECORDED

Called back to record response -- I should have been doing this all along...
Lynnete x8330 (or just 330)
Workorder # 1234381 (10/4)
Workorder # 1223416 (10/6)
TIME IS NOW scheduled for 10/4 TOMORROW: 8a-8pm

Sun 10/3/99 12:17p (9 min) RECORDED

(couldn't make out name) x8265
Called back to double-check -- appts still have same times
Credits applied to acct:
$17.76 - no record of why
$15.84 - no record of why
$20.00 - missed appt (credited 9/29)
$20.00 - missed appt (credited 9/30)
$11.66 - expressnet (credited 9/29)
$10.00 - 9/23 -- self install kit
Apparently the person I talked with on 9/30 at 11:45a found out I was NOT lying and added another credit.

Mon 10/4/99 7:10p (12 min) RECORDED

Talked with John x8310
No record of appt for 10/4 -- only record is 10/5
I went ballistic -- he's connecting me to someone...
6 min in -- supervisor was on phone -- still holding as noone ever calls me back
7:21p -- ringing for supervisor -- no answer -- put into "admin offices closed" message and "choose an extension" mode, then disconnected!!!!

Mon 10/4/99 7:24p

I try calling back -- while on hold John calls back on house line -- I told him to have supervisor call me on work line so I can record it.

Mon 10/4/99 8:05p

NO call yet -- I call back.

Mon 10/4/99 8:12p

finally get an answer -- rep refuses to be recorded -- I ask to be connected to another rep

Mon 10/4/99 8:16p

Connected me to supervisor

Patrick x7790 (voice not his -- but his ext) RECORDED

crediting for total of 3 on-times guarantees, and free month cable & modem

Says he will make sure this gets done tomorrow

Tue 10/5/99 7:45p

Still not enough signal for modem... calling back. Left message for Patrick x7790 to call back

Tue 10/5/99 9:12p

No return call yet. Calling back...

Waited 16 min for answer on hold with crappy music

Zachary (no perm extension) - RECORDED (8:30)

Rescheduled for 10/6 -- no reason why -- rep says "line referrals give way to outages and have no priority".

Getting connected to supervisor.

Supervisors busy -- rep having them call back.

Tue 10/5/99 9:50p

Supervisor (Patrick) calls back - RECORDED (7:00)

He still seems empathetic - he's checking on status -- there were no outages for the tech assigned this job and he doesn't know why it wasn't done. He says the line ref has been rescheduled 7 times.

Got address & name to send complaint to VP @ Cable TV Montgomery.

Wed 10/6/99

Techs supposedly boosted the signal at MD route 355 -- that's a Helluva long way away. If the problem was really out there, there must have been hundreds or thousands of signal complaints...

Modem signal still too weak. No noticeable difference in TV picture either.

Tomorrow scheduled for new outlet installation -- I'm calling tonight to see if the installer can check the signal to the modem before installing, or it's not worth installing a new outlet.

Wed 10/6/99 8:50p

8:55 -- rep refuses to be taped -- I ask to speak with supervisor -- after several minutes rep returns and says supervisors busy.

Supervisor supposedly calling back.

Wed 10/6/99 10:05p

No callback yet -- calling in again. Normal really bad music and brief announcements RE wait.

10:12 -- connected to message that all busy -- leave message or wait -- I think the "wait" option just re-called the "service" number and put me back in at the END of the queue. At least that's what it sounded like based on the ring and initial answer.

10:20 -- connected to same message that all busy -- put BACK at end of queue again

10:28 -- connected to same message again -- put BACK AT END OF QUEUE AGAIN

10:36 -- connected to same message -- left message to cancel appt

11:13 -- no call back yet

Thur 10/7/99 6:35a

Called and canceled install appt (RECORDED)

Will call back tonight to get signal strength worked out

Thur 10/7/99

Tech stops by to check modem. It magically works. I tried several times the night before...

Now I need to reschedule the outlet installation. Bad timing...

Fri 10/8/99  11:00a

Called to reschedule appt -- rep refused to be recorded, put me on hold -- several minutes later I was disconnected

Fri 10/8/99 11:11a

Called back to reschedule appt.

11:19 connected -- rep said ok to record, but would not give name (gave ext)

Scheduling appt...

For tomorrow, 10/9/99 9a-12p

Workorder: 1249180

Fri 10/8/99 2:45p

cable out

on then off again @ 2:52p

up by 3:05p

off @ 3:28p

on @ 3:30p

Sat 10/9/99 8:15a

Installers arrive 45 min early (sched for 9a). Installed a new line split off the bedroom, through the attic, down through the closet in the office. Works fine.

Main installer really seemed competent -- I've never seen a cable tech who actually sounded like he knew what he was doing and how to properly balance the signal through splitters. His assistant seemed new and he was training him.

Sun 10/10/99 9:50a

cable out (last time I was online was around 930a) 

still out at 1041, noon, 1250,  120p, 208p

Back on by 3:25p

Total outage: between 4:38-5:55 total hours

Mon 10/11/99 10:55a

cable just went out

(Nancy says it's gone out everyday as far as she can remember, for at least a few minutes

Back at 11:25a

Back out at 12:10p

Back on at 12:15p

Fri 10/29/99 10:30a

Cable is out again. Last night it was so snowy I couldn't get a connection. Cable is "aware of the problem" and will send somone on Tuesday, 11/2 between 2 and 5 pm. (order #1286514)

over the past few weeks, we've gotten several short outages (usually under an hour).

Tue 11/02/99

Cable's been pretty iffy over the past couple weeks -- no modem outage since Friday though.

Surprise, surprise, no show...

Calling to check and ask for credit... Perhaps I'll never need to pay for cable service...

8:35p -- Spoke with someone who said the call was cancelled on 10/29, and insisted that I was called about it. I never received any call 

Said she cannot give me the $20 credit, but will write a note for a supervisor to look into it.

I told her the picture still sucks, and she rescheduled me for Nov 10 (!!!), 8-11a. (Order #1294421)

I asked for permission to tape record a confirmation and she declined. I asked to speak to a supervisor but none is available. She put me back in the queue to try again.

8:50p - got another person -- she refused to be taped, claiming it's illegal. (I told her it's legal as long as both parties are aware and consent to the taping.) Then she said I'm not listed on the account. I had Nancy talk with her to straighten that out.

She's going to have a supervisor call me... Let's see if that ever happens...

Supervisor Patrick (yea!) calls about an hour later. Gives me the credit and tells me that they're having some big meetings RE "customer service". Also -- he allows me to record the conversation, verifying the appt for Nov 10 at 7:30a. Surprise! The previous CSR screwed up... He switched it to 8a.

Mon 11/08/99

Cable's been off & on all day -- lots of quick flashes of snow (really annoying) 

Went out at 12:15p, still out at 1230p, 1240p, 1p, 115p

Called cable to report -- CSR said they'd give me a 1 day credit. (Actually a nice person for once... Perhaps Patrick's talk helped?)

out again at 140p, on at 150p

Out again at 430p -- keeps flashing off/on. Can't keep a net connection...

Fri 11/12/98

2:00p - cable going off/on...

still problem at 215p
